The Best Movie's According to Isaac

My top 3 movies of all time:

Coraline

I love this movie because of the whimsical eerieness, animation style, and
exploration of curiosity that it envokes.

The Joker

I like this movie because of the alternative and open ended, imagination envoking
story. This resenbles the same feeling as fight club where all scenes are open for
Interpretation. I connected with this because the Joker is seen as an outcast and
and avoided because of uncontrollable mental illness. This leads him to be shut off
and abused by society until he reaches an intense boiling point.

I am Legend

I like this movie because it intesely pulls you into this post-apocalyptic
world. You feel for the main character and see all he has lost and all he has.
You watch as he try to cling on to any hope of connection and humanity.
